About me

I am Nam Jiho. I was born on September 23, 1999, and I grew up in South Korea. However, I am currently living in Toronto. I am studying computer programming at Seneca College. My interest in computers and programming started when I was young, and I have been passionately studying ever since. I find infinite possibilities and joy in solving problems and creating through programming. I have a particular interest in paying attention to details and creating efficient and robust software. Additionally, I value collaboration and communication, actively participating in team projects and striving for smooth collaboration with team members. I will continue to pursue self-development and embrace new technologies and challenges. My goal is to contribute to society and create innovative solutions through computer programming.

why I chose this game

I chose to create a website about StarCraft because it holds a special place in my heart. When I was younger, I used to play StarCraft with my friends frequently, and it became a cherished memory of our time together. The game not only provided us with hours of fun and excitement but also strengthened our bond as we strategized and competed against each other. Beyond my personal connection, StarCraft is a game that has garnered a massive following worldwide. It has a passionate community of players who love and appreciate the depth and complexity of the gameplay. Knowing that so many people around the globe share the same enthusiasm for the game makes it even more special to me. Moreover, StarCraft has become an iconic part of gaming history. Its influence on the real-time strategy genre and esports cannot be understated. The game's competitive scene has produced legendary players and unforgettable moments, further solidifying its status as a gaming classic. By creating this website, I hope to share my love for StarCraft with others and provide a platform where both veterans and newcomers can come together to learn, discuss, and relive the excitement of this incredible game. Whether you're a seasoned player or someone curious about the game's legacy, I aim to provide valuable information and foster a sense of community around this timeless masterpiece.
